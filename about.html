About the Project

This project seeks to generate novel genomic resources to enhance species delimitation, genetic diversity assessment, and monitoring of Kenyan corals‚Äîkey steps in boosting the success and adaptability of ongoing reef restoration programs.

We propose a cost-effective, tiered sequencing strategy combining low-coverage whole genome sequencing (lcWGS) and Restriction-site Associated DNA sequencing (RADseq), with long-read sequencing incorporated for a representative subset to improve marker resolution.

These technologies will enable identification of species-informative SNPs and development of novel DNA barcodes, directly supporting coral taxonomy and conservation efforts.
